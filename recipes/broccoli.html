Cheesy Broccoli

Description

Yummy cheesy broccoli side

Ingredients

  • 1 lb broccoli
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup shredded cheddar
  • 2 Tbsp grated parmesan
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • ground mustard to taste
  • cayenne to taste

Steps

  1. place the broccoli in a pan with enough water to cover the bottom
  2. high heat until the water starts boiling then cover the pan and simmer for approximately 5 minutes
  3. in another pan add the butter until it melts then sprinkle the flour until golden
  4. slowly add the milk stirring constantly, simmer until it thickens
  5. slowly add the cheddar in until fully melted then add the rest of the ingredients
  6. pour the cheese sauce over the broccoli and enjoy
